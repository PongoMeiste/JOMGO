Welcome to JOMGO!

We’re Jake, Joseph, and Mason, three friends who came together to create a fun collection of clicker games. Our goal is simple: to bring you engaging, easy-to-play games that you can enjoy in your free time.

At JOMGO, we’ve developed three exciting games: Waxed Lightly Weathered Cut Copper Stairs, Car Clicker, and Dirt Clicker. Each offers a unique experience designed to keep you hooked for hours.

Waxed Lightly Weathered Cut Copper Stairs

If you love random clicker games with no point and no purpose, come and play Waxed Lightly Weathered Cut Copper Stairs! Click on a Waxed Lightly Weathered Cut Copper Stair to collect shards and unlock workers for more fun!

Dirt Clicker

Dirt Clicker revolves around the greatest block in Minecraft: dirt! Upgrade tools, buy workers, and gather as much dirt as you can in this fun clicker game.

Car Clicker

Car Clicker is a fast-paced game where you earn points by clicking on cars, unlocking mechanics, racers, and creating a car empire!
